Everything in one place

The back office, reimagined for the line.

Six tools most restaurants juggle across five apps. Stationly replaces spreadsheets, sticky notes, and the group chat.

Tablet time clock

PIN clock‑in on a shared tablet. No apps for staff. Live shift timers, overtime flags, and break tracking built in.

Smart scheduling

Drag‑drop shifts, see labor cost in real time, and text the finished schedule to staff with one click.

Invoices & AP

Photograph a vendor invoice. Stationly extracts line items, codes to the right GL, and flags price changes.

Costs & P&L

Prime cost, food cost, labor ratio — all live. Compare against targets and get weekly AI briefings on what to fix.

Inspection‑ready

Temperature logs, cleaning checklists, and license tracking. Walk into your DBPR inspection without the scramble.

Recipe costing

Know what every plate actually costs. When invoice prices change, your recipe margins update automatically.

How it works

Three steps to running on Stationly.

Connect your data

Import staff, menu, and sales from Toast, Square, or a CSV. Takes 10 minutes — we do the mapping.

Set up the tablet

Stand a tablet at the entrance. Staff type their PIN to clock in. No app install, no training.

Run the business

Each morning, open the briefing. See labor, food cost, compliance, and what needs your attention — all in one place.
